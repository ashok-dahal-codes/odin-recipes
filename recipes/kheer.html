kheer

Ingredient

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup basmati rice

Steps

  1. Rinse the basmati rice in water a couple of times, then soak it in water for 30 minutes. After soaking, drain the water.
  2. in a heavy-bottomed pan, pour the milk and bring it to a boil over medium heat. Stir occasionally to prevent the milk from sticking to the bottom of the pan.
